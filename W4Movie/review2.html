The Dark Knight Rises takes place years after the previous movie, showing Bruce Wayne as older, weaker, and no longer active as Batman. Gotham seems peaceful at first, but that changes when Bane arrives and takes control of the city. Unlike the Joker, Bane uses physical strength and strategy to break Gotham down, making him a very different kind of threat. A big part of Dark Knight Rises is Bruce rebuilding himself after losing everything. After being defeated and thrown into prison, he has to find the strength to come back and face Bane again. The movie ends with Batman saving Gotham and finally getting the chance to move on from his life as a hero. It wraps up the story by showing that even after everything falls apart, it’s possible to rebuild and start fresh.
