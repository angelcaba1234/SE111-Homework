In The Dark Knight, the story goes way deeper than just Batman fighting criminals. The movie is mainly about chaos versus order, mainly through the Joker, who doesn’t care about money or power and just wants to prove that people are easy to break. Watching him go up against Batman makes the whole movie feel tense, because you never really know what’s going to blow up next. What makes The Dark Knight stand out in my opinion is the character development, especially Harvey Dent. He starts off as a symbol of hope for Gotham, but after everything he goes through, he completely changes to evil. Batman ends up taking the blame for Dent’s actions to protect the city’s hope, which is what a true hero would do.
